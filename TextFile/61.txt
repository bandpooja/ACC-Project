Visa Sponsorship: Yes Relocation Assistance: Yes Technologies gophpdockerkubernetesweb Job description Introduction Our Mission Mercari is a Japan-based company with the mission of “create value in a global marketplace where anyone can buy & sell.” Having continuously worked toward this goal since our founding in 2013, we are now transforming from a startup into a global player. While this transformation brings its own specific set of challenges, we believe that solving them one after another will allow us to fully achieve our mission. Are you interested in working together to take on these exciting challenges? Our Culture Mercari’s culture has been central to the company since our founding, with the three core values of Go Bold, All for One, and Be a Pro. In addition, the “Trust & Openness” value was introduced to embody our culture of mutual trust, where information remains transparent and rules are kept to a minimum. Regardless of how big Mercari gets, our culture is essential to achieving our mission and will be promoted throughout our organization. Position Overview Work Responsibilities Merlogi, Inc. is a Mercari Group company established on October 28, 2021, with the mission of “Relaying value far and wide for kinder living.” With our unique data and technology, we aim to provide logistics services kinder to all people involved and to society as a whole. We’re working to expand Mercari Posts and other Mercari touchpoints to more locations and build a package pickup distribution network, creating an even better experience for Mercari’s more than 20 million monthly users. The main components of the role are as follows: Design, develop and test exciting new features iteratively using a data-driven mindset. Take full ownership (as a team) of a large domain comprising many codebases impacting millions of Mercari users. Learn domain knowledge, technical skills, and industry disciplines with the aim to teach and continuously improve the team as a whole. Be on-call for several production systems. Bold Challenges We are looking for people that are interested in our services, mission, and values, and want to work where engineers can go bold, use the latest technology, make autonomous decisions, and take on challenges at a rapid pace. Required Experience Shared understanding and belief in our company’s mission and values Strong backend experience working with distributed systems in the cloud. Proven ability to quickly learn new technologies and skills if not already familiar. Professional engineering experience with consumer applications. Demonstrated ability to write clean-code with appropriate tests. Preferred Experience Strong experience working in large Go (or PHP) codebases Strong experience on relational databases. Experience with Docker and Kubernetes or similar systems Technical or team leadership experience in cross-functional teams. Strong sense of software engineering (Design, security and ownership) for product. Web (React) or mobile (native iOS/Android) experience is a plus.
